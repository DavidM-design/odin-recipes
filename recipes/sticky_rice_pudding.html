Sticky Rice Pudding

Description

A gluten-free, vegan Thai sticky rice pudding with coconut cream. Sticky rice pudding is a dessert found on many continents, especially in Asia where rice is a staple. This recipe is based off of a Thai sticky rice pudding with coconut cream.

Ingredients

Yield: 3 servings

  • For Coconut Cream
    • 3/4 cup low-fat milk
    • 4 dates
    • 1 tbsp cooked sticky rice
  • For Sticky Rice
    • 1/2 cup glutinous sticy rice
    • 3 dates
    • 3/4 cup water
  • For Topping
    • 1 cup mango
    • 3 tsp white sesame seeds

Steps

  1. To Make Coconut Cream

    2. Deseed and soak dates in warm water for 10 minutes (or more if still dry). Discard the water and add dates with coconut milk to a blender. Blend till well mixed. To make the mixture creamier add one tbsp of cooked sticky rice. Refrigerate (or keep aside if serving at room temperature).

  2. To Make Rice

    3. Soak rice for at least two hours or overnight. The longer you soak the rice, the softer and stickier it becomes. Before cooking the rice, wash soaked rice several times, till the water stops being milky liquid.

  3. To Serve

    4. Add 1/3 cup of hot, steaming sticky rice to a dish; top with coconut cream and cold mango cubes. Top with toasted seeds or nuts or your choice. Enjoy.
